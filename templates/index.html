What does flange pressure rating mean?
The flange rating denotes the maximum pressure a flange can sustain at high temperatures, and it rises proportionally with the flange's capacity to tolerate higher pressures and temperatures. Selecting the appropriate flange rating is imperative for ensuring optimal performance and safety under specified usage and operating conditions.

How to choose the suitable rating for application?
I. Determine the maximum working temperature of the system and select it from dropdown options.
II. Select the appropriate material grade that corresponds to your system's requirements.
III. Output result will indicate the maximum pressure that each flange rating (Class) can withstand !!
